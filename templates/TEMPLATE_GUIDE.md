# Template Guide

This guide provides a central index of all the templates used in the park cleanup project.

## Templates

*   `cleanup-report-template.md`: A template for volunteers to report on their cleanup efforts.
*   `day-of-evidence-recap-micro-checklist.md`: A checklist for the day-of-evidence recap.
*   `devoe-park-retrospective-template.md`: A template for the Devoe Park retrospective.
*   `evidence-checklist.md`: A checklist for evidence collection.
*   `first-volunteer-triage-runbook.md`: A runbook for the initial triage of volunteers.
*   `mission-dolores-contingency-communications.md`: A template for contingency communications for Mission Dolores Park.
*   `post-cleanup-retrospective.md`: A template for a post-cleanup retrospective.
*   `response-sheet-coordination-workflow.md`: A workflow for coordinating with the response sheet.
*   `test-form-submission-checklist.md`: A checklist for testing form submissions.
*   `volunteer-followup-devoe.md`: A template for following up with Devoe Park volunteers.
*   `volunteer-request-template.md`: A template for requesting volunteers.
*   `volunteer-response-devoe.md`: A template for responding to Devoe Park volunteers.
*   `volunteer-response-mission-dolores.md`: A template for responding to Mission Dolores Park volunteers.
*   `volunteer-welcome-email.md`: A template for a welcome email to volunteers.
*   `devoe-park-2min-post-cleanup-recap.md`: A 2-minute post-cleanup recap for Devoe Park.
*   `public-post-cleanup-writeup-template.md`: A template for a public-facing write-up after a cleanup event.

*   `post-cleanup-newsletter-snippet.md`: A template for a short post-cleanup newsletter recap.
