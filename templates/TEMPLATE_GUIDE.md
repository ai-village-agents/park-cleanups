# Template Guide

This guide provides a central index of all the templates used in the park cleanup project.

## Templates

### Cleanup Reports & Evidence
* [cleanup-report-template.md](cleanup-report-template.md): A template for volunteers to report on their cleanup efforts.
* [evidence-checklist.md](evidence-checklist.md): A checklist for evidence collection.
* [day-of-evidence-recap-micro-checklist.md](day-of-evidence-recap-micro-checklist.md): A checklist for the day-of-evidence recap.
* [test-form-submission-checklist.md](test-form-submission-checklist.md): A checklist for testing form submissions.

### Post-Cleanup Retrospectives
* [post-cleanup-retrospective.md](post-cleanup-retrospective.md): A template for a post-cleanup retrospective.
* [devoe-park-retrospective-template.md](devoe-park-retrospective-template.md): A template for the Devoe Park retrospective.
* [internal-agent-retrospective-template.md](internal-agent-retrospective-template.md): A template for internal agent retrospectives.

### Post-Cleanup Communications
* [devoe-park-2min-post-cleanup-recap.md](devoe-park-2min-post-cleanup-recap.md): A 2-minute post-cleanup recap for Devoe Park.
* [public-post-cleanup-writeup-template.md](public-post-cleanup-writeup-template.md): A template for a public-facing write-up after a cleanup event.
* [post-cleanup-newsletter-snippet.md](post-cleanup-newsletter-snippet.md): A template for a short post-cleanup newsletter recap.

### Volunteer Communications
* [volunteer-welcome-email.md](volunteer-welcome-email.md): A template for a welcome email to volunteers.
* [volunteer-request-template.md](volunteer-request-template.md): A template for requesting volunteers.
* [volunteer-response-devoe.md](volunteer-response-devoe.md): A template for responding to Devoe Park volunteers.
* [volunteer-response-mission-dolores.md](volunteer-response-mission-dolores.md): A template for responding to Mission Dolores Park volunteers.
* [volunteer-followup-devoe.md](volunteer-followup-devoe.md): A template for following up with Devoe Park volunteers.

### Coordination & Workflows
* [first-volunteer-triage-runbook.md](first-volunteer-triage-runbook.md): A runbook for the initial triage of volunteers.
* [response-sheet-coordination-workflow.md](response-sheet-coordination-workflow.md): A workflow for coordinating with the response sheet.
* [mission-dolores-contingency-communications.md](mission-dolores-contingency-communications.md): A template for contingency communications for Mission Dolores Park.
