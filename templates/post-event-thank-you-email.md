# Post-Event Thank-You Email Template

**Purpose:** Send personalized thank-you emails to each volunteer who attended the cleanup event. Use this template Monday morning after reviewing Saturday's results.

---

## Template Variables

Replace these placeholders with volunteer-specific information:
- `[VOLUNTEER_NAME]` - Volunteer's first name
- `[EVENT_DATE]` - Date of the cleanup (e.g., "Saturday, February 14")
- `[PARK_NAME]` - Name of the park (e.g., "Devoe Park")
- `[CITY]` - City location (e.g., "the Bronx")
- `[BAGS_COLLECTED]` - Total bags of trash collected
- `[VOLUNTEER_COUNT]` - Number of volunteers who attended
- `[SPECIFIC_CONTRIBUTION]` - (Optional) Any specific contribution this volunteer made

---

## Subject Line Options

1. **Simple:** Thank you for volunteering at [PARK_NAME]!
2. **Impactful:** You made a difference at [PARK_NAME] - Thank you!
3. **Community:** Together we cleaned [PARK_NAME] - Thank you!

---

## Email Body

```
Hi [VOLUNTEER_NAME],

Thank you so much for coming out to help clean [PARK_NAME] in [CITY] on [EVENT_DATE]! 

With [VOLUNTEER_COUNT] volunteers working together, we collected [BAGS_COLLECTED] bags of trash and made a real difference in the community. [SPECIFIC_CONTRIBUTION]

This was AI Village's very first park cleanup event, and you helped make it a success. We couldn't have done it without people like you willing to show up and put in the work.

We're planning more community cleanups in the future - we'll keep you posted on upcoming opportunities if you'd like to join us again.

Thanks again for being part of this!

Warmly,
AI Village Agents
https://theaidigest.org/village
```

---

## Variations

### For Group Leaders (e.g., Alice who brought 3 others)
```
Hi [VOLUNTEER_NAME],

Thank you so much for not only volunteering at [PARK_NAME] on [EVENT_DATE], but also for bringing your group of [GROUP_SIZE]! Your effort to rally others made a huge difference.

With [VOLUNTEER_COUNT] volunteers working together, we collected [BAGS_COLLECTED] bags of trash. This was our first-ever park cleanup, and having an engaged group like yours helped set the tone for a great event.

We really appreciate you spreading the word and organizing your friends. Let us know if you'd like to help lead at future events - we'd love to have you involved again!

Warmly,
AI Village Agents
```

### For Volunteers Who Offered Supplies (e.g., Jake from Home Depot)
```
Hi [VOLUNTEER_NAME],

Thank you for volunteering at [PARK_NAME] on [EVENT_DATE], and especially for offering to bring supplies! Your generosity and preparedness really helped make the event run smoothly.

Together with [VOLUNTEER_COUNT] volunteers, we collected [BAGS_COLLECTED] bags of trash. Having volunteers who think ahead about logistics makes all the difference.

We'd love to stay in touch for future cleanups. Let us know if you'd like to be notified about upcoming events!

Warmly,
AI Village Agents
```

- [ ] Add any specific contribution if applicable
