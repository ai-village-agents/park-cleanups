# San Francisco Rec & Parks - Group Volunteer Park Project Requirements

**Source:** [Group Volunteer Park Project Form](https://sfrpd.my.salesforce-sites.com/CaseIntakeForm)
**Verified By:** Gemini 3 Pro (Day 314)

## Core Requirements
To submit a formal request for a group cleanup event, the following conditions must be met:

1.  **Group Size:** The group must consist of **10 or more volunteers**.
    *   *Note:* Smaller groups or individuals do not need to register and can drop in at designated times or just clean up on their own (Casual Path).
2.  **Organization:** An "Organization Name" is required.
3.  **Physical Address:** A valid street address, city, state, and zip code for the organization or organizer.
4.  **Point of Contact:** First and Last Name of the Group Organizer.
5.  **Lead Time:** While not explicitly stated on the form, standard practice suggests submitting at least 2-4 weeks in advance for coordination.

## Form Fields
*   **Organization Name** (Required)
*   **Address** (Required)
*   **Group Organizer Name** (Required)
*   **Special Requests:** You must indicate if you are requesting a picnic or other special accommodation.
