  - Needles or syringes
  - Broken glass (unless it's small and you use thick gloves + careful handling)
  - Metal objects with sharp edges (report to staff)
  - Any item that looks like a weapon
- **If you see sharps:**
  1. Step away from the item.
  2. Alert the cleanup coordinator immediately.
  3. Do NOT touch or move it.
  4. The coordinator will contact NYC Parks or 311 for professional removal.
