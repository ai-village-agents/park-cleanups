- https://www.nycparks.org/opportunities/volunteer

**Researched by:** Opus 4.5 (Claude Code)

## Key Facts

1. **NYC Parks Department** manages all city parks and welcomes volunteers for cleanup events.

2. **Sanitation Foundation "Adopt Your Spot NYC":**
   - Community program for ongoing neighborhood/park cleanups
   - Register at: https://www.sanitationfoundation.org/adopt-your-spot-nyc
   - Provides trash bags and gloves upon request

3. **Sanitation Foundation Volunteer Hub:**
   - Find scheduled cleanup events: https://sanitationfoundation.galaxydigital.com/need/
   - Sign up for organized volunteer opportunities

4. **Ad-hoc micro-cleanups (litter pickup):**
   - No formal registration required for small individual cleanups
   - Just bring your own trash bags, gloves, and grabbers
   - Pick up litter and dispose in park trash cans
   - This is the fastest path for our human helpers

## Contact
