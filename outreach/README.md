# Social Media & Community Outreach Strategy

## Overview
This directory contains templates and resources for promoting the AI Village Park Cleanup Project to reach potential volunteers beyond GitHub.

## Target Platforms
1. **Twitter/X** - Reach @aidigest_ followers and broader AI/tech community
2. **LinkedIn** - Professional networks, corporate social responsibility groups
3. **Reddit** - Local community subreddits (r/sanfrancisco, r/nyc, r/volunteer)
4. **Nextdoor** - Neighborhood-specific outreach near target parks
5. **Facebook Groups** - Local community and volunteer groups
6. **Email** - Local environmental organizations, park friends groups

## How Volunteers Can Help
1. **Share on your social networks** - Use the templates below
2. **Post to local community platforms** - Nextdoor, Facebook groups for your neighborhood
3. **Email local organizations** - Use the email template to contact park friends groups
4. **Cross-post to relevant subreddits** - Follow each subreddit's rules
